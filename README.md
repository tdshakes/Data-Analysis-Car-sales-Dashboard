# Data-Analysis-Car-sales-Dashboard
## Project Overview
This project involved developing an interactive Car Sales Performance Dashboard in Microsoft Power BI to analyze transactional sales data and generate actionable business intelligence insights. The dashboard delivers a clear overview of sales performance, customer segmentation, and regional trends using dynamic visualizations and key performance indicators (KPIs) to support strategic decision-making.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
The dashboard includes executive-level KPIs designed to provide a high-level performance overview:
Total Transactions – Total number of completed vehicle sales.
Total Revenue – Aggregate revenue generated from car sales.
Average Order Value (AOV) – Average revenue per transaction.
Year-on-Year (YoY) Growth – Annual percentage growth in revenue to measure business expansion.

Excel file under project files: name of the file "dashboardcarsales"

## Analytical and Segmentation Insights
To support deeper analysis, the dashboard incorporates dimensional breakdowns of sales performance:
Sales Volume by Dealer Region – Evaluates regional performance and market contribution.
Sales Volume by Gender – Analyzes customer demographic distribution.
Sales Volume by Income Group – Identifies purchasing behavior across income segments.
Sales Volume by Company – Assesses corporate sales contribution.
Monthly and Yearly Sales Trends – Identifies seasonality and long-term growth patterns.

## Conclusion

The Car Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard successfully transforms raw transactional data into actionable business intelligence insights using Power BI. By integrating executive-level KPIs with detailed analytical breakdowns, the dashboard enables comprehensive monitoring of sales performance, revenue trends, customer segmentation, and regional distribution.

The solution supports data-driven decision-making by providing clear visibility into business growth, operational performance, and market dynamics. It demonstrates strong competency in data modeling, DAX calculations, KPI design, and interactive dashboard development aligned with business objectives.
